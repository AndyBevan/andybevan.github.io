I’m restarting this blog on Astro because the old setup tangled styling iteration with tooling I didn’t enjoy maintaining. The new stack keeps prose in Markdown, lets me drop interactive islands by default, and gives me a polished layout without sacrificing performance.

The goal is to write long-form technical articles, highlight experiments, and eventually embed playful demos or small utilities. AstroPaper already embraces responsive components, SEO-friendly structure, and Tailwind-powered motion, which means I can focus on the writing again.

You’ll find a hidden theme showcase page where I can swap between curated themes while I decide what direction to lock in. Once the palette is settled, the rest of the world just sees the chosen look.
