This project is still the kind of place where I experiment with gradients, tactile motion, and the balance between clarity and atmosphere. Each layout iteration surfaces a different rhythm: some lean into wide serif spreads, others hide delicate motion behind user pivots.

The plan is to document that process in technical essays, share notes about tooling, and eventually drop modular widgets that demonstrate the systems I’m proud to build. AstroPaper’s editorial tone gives me a strong baseline for that work.
